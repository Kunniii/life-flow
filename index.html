What is Life Flow?

Life Flow is a web application designed to assist individuals with ADHD in remaining organized and focused. The app is built using cutting-edge technologies like ReactJS, Vite, and Tailwind CSS, making it both speedy and user-friendly. To use Life Flow, follow these steps:

  • Create a list of tasks in Markdown format. Tasks starts with 
    - (dash)
    is consider not completed
  • Add your tasks to the app.
  • Prioritize your tasks based on their importance.
  • Set reminders to help you stay on track (in future updates, integrated Clock will be added).
  • Hit the green "RUN" button or use the shortcut 
    Ctrl + Enter
    to view your tasks.
  • Your tasks will be displayed in a clear and simple format, making it easy to stay on top of your to-do list.

Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply someone who has difficulty focusing, Life Flow is an ideal solution for keeping your tasks organized and staying productive.
